Events

Robocon Participation

The Robotics Forum (TRF) consistently competes in Robocon, an international robotics competition. They showcased their robots in the National DD Robocon 2024 League, emphasizing precision and teamwork. Learn more

Level-1 Workshop 2024

In early 2024, TRF conducted the Level-1 Workshop, introducing students to robotics fundamentals. This workshop provided hands-on experience and foundational knowledge for aspiring roboticists. Learn more

Vishwa Tech Spectra (VTS) Participation

TRF participates in Vishwa Tech Spectra (VTS), a three-day inter-college tech fest at VIT Pune. The event features technical competitions, workshops, and challenges, including robotics contests.

Collaborative Workshops

TRF collaborates with other technical forums and industry experts to host workshops on topics like machine learning, automation, and advanced robotics, enhancing members' technical skills.

Alumni Interaction Sessions

Regular sessions with TRF alumni working in robotics and related fields provide current members with insights into industry trends, career guidance, and networking opportunities.
