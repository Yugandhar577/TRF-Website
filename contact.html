Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Robotics Forum?

The Robotics Forum (TRF) is the premier robotics club at VIT Pune, focusing on robotics innovation, training, and competitions.

How can I join TRF?

You can join by attending our workshops or contacting us via email or social media.

Do you offer workshops?

Yes, TRF organizes multiple workshops throughout the year for beginners and advanced learners.
