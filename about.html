The Robotics Forum (TRF) - VIT Pune

Premier Robotics Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune

About Us

The Robotics Forum (TRF) is the premier robotics club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) Pune, boasting a rich legacy of over 16 years. Established in 2006, TRF is a vibrant community of dedicated robotics enthusiasts from various engineering disciplines, all united by a shared passion for advancing robotic technology.

Vision

To lead innovation and excellence in robotics, creating a community of passionate individuals dedicated to advancing robotic technology.

Mission

To provide a platform for robotics enthusiasts to develop technical skills, teamwork, and communication; explore creative ideas; and participate in competitions, workshops, and projects that enhance their knowledge and contribute to their personal and professional growth.

Workshops and Training

TRF is committed to fostering technical skills among students through a variety of workshops, including Level-1, RoboDroid, RoboVision, Level-2, and CAD Modelling. These sessions cover essential topics such as sensor integration, motor control, embedded C programming, microcontrollers, computer vision, app development, and physical modeling tools. To date, TRF has trained over 3,500 members, continually expanding its reach each year.

Domains

  • Electronics: Focuses on developing and researching circuit schematics and PCBs, analyzing sensors, programming robots, and actuating them through integrated systems.
  • Mechanics: Responsible for designing structurally sound robots, laying the groundwork for their creation, and ensuring robust mechanical frameworks.
  • Software: Engages in areas such as Android development, image processing, machine learning, and web design, undertaking research and industrial projects to delve deeper into these fields.
